## v1.3.0 — Major UI Overhaul & Enhanced User Experience

**Release Date:** May 28, 2026

### 🎨 UI & Design Enhancements

**Legal & Information Pages**
- ✅ New **About Fr. CRCE** page with college info, departments, quick facts, and social media links
- ✅ Redesigned **Privacy Policy** with collapsible sections and icons
- ✅ New **Terms & Conditions** page with 10 detailed collapsible sections
- ✅ Enhanced **About page** with team development cards, role badges, and Special Thanks section
- ✅ Reorganized legal links for better discoverability

**Development Team Display**
- ✅ Individual developer cards with avatar initials and role badges
- ✅ Color-coded roles: Core Developers (red), UI Team (purple), Contributors (green)
- ✅ Social links (GitHub, LinkedIn, website) integrated on cards
- ✅ Collapsible Development Team section for cleaner layout

### 🛒 Shopping & Checkout Improvements

**Cart & Checkout Flow**
- ✅ New two-step checkout process: Review items → Select payment method → Checkout
- ✅ Payment method selector with UPI (PhonePe) and Pay at Counter options
- ✅ UPI logo display for better payment method identification

**Item Details & Menu**
- ✅ Cart quantity sync on Item Detail page — "Add to Cart" button transforms into quantity selector
- ✅ Real-time price updates as quantity changes
- ✅ Unavailable items sorted to bottom of menu for better UX
- ✅ Cart badge shows total quantity in real-time

**Payment Experience**
- ✅ Completely redesigned Payment page with clearer layout
- ✅ Order summary card with itemized breakdown
- ✅ PhonePe branding and security badge
- ✅ Improved loading states

### 📱 Order Management

**Order Details Page**
- ✅ Removed queue positioning (simplified UX)
- ✅ "Ready" status now shows in green
- ✅ Order number badge displayed for unpaid orders
- ✅ Transaction ID truncated for better readability with copy button
- ✅ Fixed multi-zone QR code support
- ✅ Critical bug fixes: Text node crashes, conditional hooks errors

**Transaction History**
- ✅ Improved Transaction ID display with copy functionality
- ✅ Cleaner layout without duplicate information

### 🖥️ Responsive Design

- ✅ Large screen support (tablets) — uses optimized background image for screens ≥ 768px wide

### 🐛 Bug Fixes

- ✅ Fixed stray text node crash on order details
- ✅ Fixed hooks rendered conditionally error
- ✅ Fixed duplicate style blocks causing syntax errors
- ✅ Improved error handling and validation

### 📊 Technical Changes

| Component | Changes |
