## v1.4.0 — Smart Scheduling, Order Auto-Expiry & Canteen Status

**Release Date:** May 28, 2026

---

### ✨ What's New

**Schedule Orders Like a Pro** 📅
- 🗓️ **Smart Date Picker** — Automatically skips Sundays (canteen closed)
- ⏰ **Smart Time Picker** — Limited to canteen hours (Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 4 PM)
- 🎯 **Smart Defaults** — Suggests the next available time slot
- ✅ **No Invalid Orders** — Can't accidentally order when canteen is closed

**Auto-Expiring Orders** ⏳
- ⏱️ **20-Minute Window** — Unpaid orders automatically expire after 20 minutes
- 📊 **Cleaner Queue** — No stale orders clogging up the system
- ✅ **Fresh Slots** — More opportunities for others to place orders
- 🔄 **No Surprises** — You'll know exactly how long you have to pay

**Real-Time Canteen Status** 🏪
- 🟢 **Live Updates** — See instantly when the canteen opens or closes
- 🚫 **Smart Blocks** — Can't order when the canteen is closed
- 📢 **Clear Banner** — Red notification shows you when the canteen isn't taking orders
- ℹ️ **No Confusion** — Always know the canteen's status

### 🎯 Why This Matters

**For You:**
- 📱 Never waste time ordering when the canteen is closed
- ⏰ Smart scheduling with time restrictions prevents confusion
- 🗓️ Know exactly when slots are available
- 💪 More reliable, fair ordering system for everyone

**For the Canteen:**
- 🏢 Better order management and queue control

- ⚙️ Automatic cleanup saves manual work
- 📈 Smoother operations during peak hours
